How it works

Three steps to a complete test plan.

01
Paste your AC
Paste your ticket, user story, or acceptance criteria. Full AC works best — but even a short description generates useful steps.
02
Pick your areas
Choose which testing areas matter for this ticket — functional, permissions, edge cases, UI, integrations, error handling, and more.
03
Test and export
Mark items pass, fail, or blocked as you go. Add notes, regenerate any section, and export to CSV when you're done.
What's included

Everything a test session needs.

🤖
AI-Generated from your AC
15–25 specific, grouped test cases in seconds. Edge cases, validations, break-it scenarios — all derived from what your ticket actually says.
👥
Real-Time Team Sessions
Share a 6-character code and your whole team works from the same checklist simultaneously. Pass, Fail, Blocked — all sync live.
✎
Notes Per Item
Capture context as you test — "fails on Safari only", "linked to PROJ-456". Notes export with your CSV so nothing gets lost.

Live Progress Tracking

Export to CSV
